Capstone Project - Great Learning

The Food 101 Project is an exciting and innovative initiative that focuses on the comprehensive analysis of food items through both classification and object detection. This project encompasses the exploration of 10 distinct food classes, with the goal of enhancing our understanding of these culinary delights.

Project Details:

Classification: The project involves the creation of robust classification models capable of identifying and categorizing food items accurately. These models enable us to recognize the dishes from the provided food classes with a high degree of precision.

Object Detection: In addition to classification, we delve into the fascinating realm of object detection. Our objective is to develop algorithms and models that can not only classify but also locate and outline the food items within images, providing valuable insights into their spatial distribution.
